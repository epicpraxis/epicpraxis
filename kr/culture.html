Operating Principles

Praxis’s operating principles define how we work with each other, with our users, and within our systems. They translate what we believe into what we do: the behaviors we value day to day, the traits we hire for, and the expectations we hold ourselves to. All of it is rooted in a shared commitment to ownership, clarity, and mutual respect, and a belief that how we work matters as much as what we build.
